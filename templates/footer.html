ABOUT

Extension is one of the mandated functions of the Palawan State University as stipulated in Section 2 of Republic Act No. 7818. A collaborative and purposeful activity that webs together the functional expertise of instruction, research and extension, extension service involves building partnerships with partner communities and agencies to provide capacity development programs and projects, information, communication and technology services to communities. Through extension programs, the University strengthens institutions by applying the knowledge, technology and expertise it generates from its two other functions --- instruction and research.
